S23 SERIES

As a part of their flagship Galaxy S series, Samsung Electronics created, developed, produced, and marketed a line of high-end Android smartphones known as the Samsung Galaxy S23 Series. The phones were released on February 17, 2023, after being tested and revealed in person on February 1, 2023, during the Galaxy Unpacked event.

Samsung S23

The Samsung Galaxy S23 boasts excellent specifications, such as a 3900mAh battery, a Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 CPU, and 8GB RAM paired with 128/256GB storage. A 6.1-inch Dynamic AMOLED display with an adjustable refresh rate of 120 Hz is featured on the phone. There is a triple-sensor configuration in the camera department.
